agape

a magazine of ethics

Agape is a magazine that fills an empty space in the American agora---it's a venue for young people to discuss important ethical dilemmas, without limits on acceptable discussion, expectations for their thoughts, or condescension about their ability to consider.

The fact is, young people possess the same intellectual and emotional abilities as adults. Ethical and philosophical dialogue is the bedrock of all political progress. And although young people may lack the credentials, they certainly possess the knowledge.

And the other fact is, even outside the youth sphere, serious consideration of how we ground our political beliefs is lacking. Politicians take their constituents' approval for granted, while slinging dogmatic mud against their opponents. Our belief is that the truth shall set us free, so long as we allow it to reach its fullest fruition; and the only way to do that is to talk.

Although we come at issues from a Catholic approach, we welcome all perspectives. We believe that diverse moral perspectives are necessary for full dialogue, and that, while we can recognize the moral imperative to follow the Church's teachings, we must also allow all voices equal time in the sun.

Our club ie run, written, and edited by students, and we accept submissions from all interested parties.
